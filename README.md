# 📌 Project Overview

Virasat-Namma Guide is an Android application developed to preserve and promote Karnataka’s hidden heritage sites, temples, forts, inscriptions, and historical monuments.

The application acts as a smart digital heritage guide that helps users discover nearby heritage locations, learn their historical significance, scan QR codes for hidden facts, listen to audio guides, and maintain a digital travel passport.

The app focuses on storytelling, cultural preservation, tourism awareness, and promoting pride in Karnataka’s rich heritage.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Karnataka has thousands of hidden heritage sites that are not included in major tourism platforms. Due to lack of awareness and proper information, many monuments and local stories are slowly being forgotten.

Virasat-Namma Guide solves this problem by digitally documenting historical places and providing tourists with an interactive heritage exploration experience.
